- `import/no-unassigned-import`
- `import/no-anonymous-default-export`: for functions & classes
- `import/exports-last`
- `import/group-exports`
- `react/function-component-definition`
- `react/jsx-key`
- `react/no-direct-mutation-state`
- `react/state-in-constructor`
